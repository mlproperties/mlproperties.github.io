About Us

Local property developers

ML Properties is a Worcester-based property company specialising in renovating and improving residential homes.

We purchase a small number of carefully selected properties each year, transforming tired or outdated houses into high-quality homes for future owners.

As local property professionals with years of experience in the Worcester housing market, we understand that every seller's situation is different — whatever yours is, we'll always treat you with honesty, respect and complete confidentiality.
